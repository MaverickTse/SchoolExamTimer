Big Wall Clock

A big digital wall clock with progress bar. For most of the exam occasions.

Optional Features:

  • 7-segment display
  • Text size and color customization
  • Beep on finish

Multi-CountDown Timer

Countdown timer for multiple rounds with set-once-and-forget in mind. Designed specially for IGCSE Physics practical exam.

If there is 1 round, Duration [R=1] will be used

For 2 rounds, Duration [R=1] and Duration [R=N] will be used

For 3 rounds or more, the sequence will be [R=1]→[1 < R < N]… →[R=N]

Current round number and progress bar for current round will be displayed. With optional 7-segment display.
